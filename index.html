About Mantesnic

Mantesnic Private Capital is a discreet boutique real estate investment company. As well as being a deal originator, we align our interests by co-investing alongside a select group of global investors.

Mantesnic has a distinct approach that is both dynamic and entrepreneurial. We are value-driven and opportunistic and operate on a deal-by-deal basis, covering all sectors of the real estate markets throughout the UK and continental Europe.

We are well-known for our ability to react quickly and aggressively pursue opportunities. We apply our vast depth of experience and proprietary analysis to identify investments which we consider low risk and which will also yield superior returns.

Through our numerous, long-established contacts, both with owners and real estate agents, we have access to the very best investment opportunities in the UK and continental Europe.
