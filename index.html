About Mantesnic

Mantesnic Private Capital is a discreet boutique investment and advisory company. As well as originating opportunities, we align our interests by co-investing alongside a select group of global investors.

Mantesnic has a distinct approach that is both dynamic and entrepreneurial. We are value-driven and opportunistic and operate on a deal-by-deal basis, covering sectors across EMEA.

We are well-known for our ability to react quickly and aggressively pursue opportunities. We apply our depth of experience and proprietary analysis to identify investments we consider low risk and which also yield superior returns.
